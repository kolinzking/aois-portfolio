# v16 Looking Forward

Authoring status: authored

## What You Should Carry Forward

Carry forward these rules:

1. Logs alone are not enough.
2. Trace IDs must connect signals.
3. Cardinality needs a budget.
4. Sampling and retention control cost.
5. Live telemetry backends require approval.

## What The Next Version Will Build On

`v16.5` builds on unified telemetry by tracing incident and agent-style multi-step behavior.
