# v30 Next Version Bridge

Authoring status: authored

## What This Version Unlocks

v30 completes Phase 9. AOIS-P now has:

- delivery pipeline and release controls from v28
- experiment and model-delivery tracking from v29
- internal platform patterns from v30
- reusable capability and interface catalogs
- self-service abstractions with guardrails
- release and model-delivery controls packaged as platform defaults

## Why The Next Version Exists

v31 begins Phase 10 with multimodal AOIS.

The bridge from v30 to v31 is:

```text
If AOIS-P has reusable platform controls, the next frontier is extending AOIS
to non-text inputs without losing observability, access, delivery, and platform
discipline.
```

v31 will focus on multimodal prompting, cross-modal analysis, and interface
changes for non-text signals.
