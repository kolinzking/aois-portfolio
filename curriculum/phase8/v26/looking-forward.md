# v26 Looking Forward

Authoring status: authored

## What You Should Carry Forward

Carry forward the idea that the product surface is part of AOIS operations.

The dashboard must make these visible:

- incidents
- traces
- route and registry decisions
- workflow checkpoints
- autonomy mode
- agent handoffs
- approval waits
- budget risk
- execution-boundary decisions
- stale, empty, disconnected, redaction-blocked, and accessibility-blocked states

## What The Next Version Will Build On

v27 builds on v26 by adding identity and policy-aware access.

The next question is not only what to show. It is who may see it, who may
approve it, and which tenant or team owns it.
