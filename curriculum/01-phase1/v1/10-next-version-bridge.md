# v1 Next Version Bridge

## What This Version Unlocks

`v1` gives AOIS a structured intelligence endpoint and a visible provider gate.

The service can now distinguish:

- structured contract
- deterministic baseline
- future provider execution

## Why The Next Version Exists

`v2` exists because real AI systems need routing decisions.

Once provider calls are approved, AOIS must decide:

- fast model or stronger model
- cheap path or higher quality path
- retry or fallback
- when to stop spending
- how latency budget affects route choice
