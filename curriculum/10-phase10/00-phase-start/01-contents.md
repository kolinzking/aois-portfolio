## Phase Progress

Phase 10 is authored through `v34`.

The phase now has a multimodal AOIS contract for image, audio, video, and
document inputs with media validation, consent, privacy, redaction,
accessibility, transcript, cross-modal consistency, evidence, confidence,
fallback, and policy gates. It also has an edge/offline inference contract for
central, edge-online, and offline-edge placement with device profiles, resource
budgets, cache, sync, freshness, observability buffers, update channels,
rollback, access, and release gates. It now adds an adversarial testing loop
with authorization, sanitized scenarios, synthetic targets, evidence, severity,
mitigation, and regression gates. It completes the frontier layer with
governance-verified computer-use controls for synthetic environments, human
approval, manual handoff, step preview, budgets, stop controls, rollback, audit,
redaction, operator watch, red-team clearance, release, and access gates.
