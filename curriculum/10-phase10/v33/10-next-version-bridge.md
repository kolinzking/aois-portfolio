# v33 Next Version Bridge

Authoring status: authored

## What This Version Unlocks

v33 gives AOIS-P a safe adversarial evaluation loop:

- authorized scope
- sanitized red-team cases
- synthetic targets
- threat-category mapping
- telemetry and evidence
- severity
- mitigation ownership
- regression closure

## Why The Next Version Exists

v34 adds governance verification and computer use.

The bridge from v33 to v34 is:

```text
red-team evidence shows where controls can fail; governance verification must
decide whether computer-use actions are allowed, bounded, observed, reversible,
and stoppable before anything touches a real environment
```

v34 will connect policy verification, operator approval, UI/computer-use
boundaries, audit evidence, stop controls, and rollback into the final Phase 10
frontier lesson.
