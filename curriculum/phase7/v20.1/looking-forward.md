# v20.1 Looking Forward

Authoring status: authored

## What You Should Carry Forward

AOIS now has a local agentic responder that can plan steps and account for their
cost.

Once a responder knows the expected or observed cost of a step, it can choose a
path:

- cheap model or expensive model
- read-only tool or no tool
- continue automatically or ask for approval
- gather more evidence or stop
- escalate to a human or stay in deterministic runbook mode

## What The Next Version Will Build On

v20.2 turns cost accounting into budget-aware routing policy.
