# v0.1 -> v0.2 Bridge

`v0.1` taught AOIS how to observe.

That means:

- collect machine signals
- format them
- inspect them

But observation is not diagnosis.

`v0.2` is the first attempt to move from:

`signal -> interpretation`

The first method AOIS will try is simple rules.

That matters because you need to feel both sides:

- why rules are attractive
- why rules stop scaling

Without `v0.1`, `v0.2` would be shallow.
Without `v0.2`, `v1` would feel like unexplained AI magic.
